Rules & Regulations

General Rules

  • Students must attend school in proper uniform and on time.
  • Mobile phones, smart watches, and electronic gadgets are strictly prohibited.
  • Respect for teachers, staff, and fellow students is mandatory.
  • Any form of vandalism or bullying will result in strict disciplinary action.

Attendance Policy

  • Minimum 75% attendance is required to appear for examinations.
  • Leave applications must be submitted in writing and duly signed by parents.

Examination Conduct

  • Any form of unfair means during examination will lead to disqualification.
  • All students must carry their admit cards on exam days.
