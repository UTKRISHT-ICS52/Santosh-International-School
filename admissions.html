Admissions

We welcome applications from students of all backgrounds who share our values and seek academic excellence.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Nursery to Class IX: Age-appropriate and previous school performance
  • Class XI: Based on Class X Board Results

Admission Process

  1. Fill the online application form or collect it from the school office.
  2. Submit the form with required documents.
  3. Appear for interaction/test as applicable.
  4. Final selection based on merit and availability of seats.

Documents Required

  • Birth Certificate
  • Previous Class Marksheet
  • Transfer Certificate (if applicable)
  • Passport-size Photographs
